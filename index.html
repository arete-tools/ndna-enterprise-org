How does your organization negotiate?

About this diagnostic

This instrument measures how your organization approaches commercial discussions — the systems, norms, processes, and environment that shape how negotiations happen. This is about the organization, not about you personally.

What to answer about

Answer based on what you observe happening in the commercial organization in your market. Not just your immediate team, and not what the organization says it does — what actually happens in practice.

How to answer
  • Answer based on what you observe, not what you think should happen.
  • Think about the general pattern across the organization, not one specific team or deal.
  • If you have not directly observed the situation described, answer based on your best understanding of what typically happens.
Confidentiality

Your individual responses are confidential. Results will only be presented at a group level.

Time

Under 10 minutes.

Building your organization's profile
Building your organization's profile

No single dimension dominates the organizational profile. That can mean the organization’s systems are genuinely flexible, adapting to different commercial contexts. Or it can mean the systems are inconsistently applied, with different teams, regions, or deal types operating under different norms. The question worth investigating: does the organization choose when to flex, or do different parts simply do different things?
